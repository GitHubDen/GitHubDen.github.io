Project

Children's Watch
Application

The watch your child needs!

This is a prototype application for a smartwatch designed for children between the ages 5-10. The project demonstrates some of the important feature to ensure your childs safety and the ability for parents to communicate with them. The app has entertainment features and scalability. The app was designed and built with C++ and visual basics.

Key Features

Messaging System

Parents are able to send messages to their child. To prevent young children from messaging stangers the messaging system is one way.

Calling System

Parents control the contact list for the childs safety. The child is able to call their contact list and leave voicemails.

Allowances

Parents can provide their child with allances so they may make purchases via NFC tap.

Schedule

The schedule feature allows the parent to create the childs schedule so they always stay informed.

Listen To Music

Entertainment features such as a music player are added for the watch application.
